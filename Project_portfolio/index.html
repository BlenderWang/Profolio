About Me

Typically one should've written something briefly about herself. However since I'm terrible at writing, I'm just going to mention a little something about my graphic works. My sincere apologies if you get bored.

I mainly would like to start drawing out my ideas on a piece of paper and then continue build on it with applications as Inkscape, Krita, Gimp, Illustrator and Photoshop and etc. For animation work, I'd like to have a rough storyboard to keep my process in check.
